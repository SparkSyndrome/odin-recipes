Buttered Noodles

Description

Cook pasta and add butter, salt and pepper. Top with italian seasoning to serve; that's all there is to it!

Ingredients (for 8 servings)

  • 1 (16 oz.) package of fettucine noodles
  • 6 tablespoons butter (cut into pieces)
  • 1/3 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste
  • Italian seasoning to taste

Steps

  1. Fill large pot with lightly salted water and bring to a boil. Stir in fettucine, bring back to boil, and cook over medium heat until tender, 8 to 10 minutes.
  2. Drain and return pasta to pot.
  3. Mix butter Parmesan cheese, salt and pepper into pasta until even combined.
  4. Serve and top with italian seasoning. Enjoy your meal!
