Zuppa Toscana

Description

In a large pot, brown the italian sausage while chopping up the potatoes. After the sausage is done browning, add the potatoes and cover; allow to cook for several minutes. Once the potatoes start to tender, add the chicken broth and then cover again and let simmer until the potatoes are fork tender. Next, add the spices and heavy cream and cover again. Once it starts to boil, add shredded kale and let simmer until the kale is softened.

Ingredients

  • Mild or spicy italian sausage
  • Russet potatoes
  • Kale
  • Heavy cream
  • Italian seasoning
  • Garlic powder
  • Onion powder
  • Paprika
  • Red pepper flakes
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Brown the italian sausage and chop up potatoes.
  2. After the sausage finishes browning, add potatoes and cover and allow to cook for several minutes.
  3. Once the potatoes are tender, add chicken broth and cover again and let simmer until the potatoes are fork tender.
  4. Next, add garlic and onion powder, paprika, italian seasoning, red pepper flakes, salt and pepper. Add the heavy cream as well, and cover again.
  5. Once it starts to boil, add shredded kale and let simmer until the kale is softened.
  6. Dish up and enjoy!!!
