Zuppa Toscana

Description

To make this recipe, first get out the Italian sausage, russet potatoes, kale, heavy cream and spices. In a large pot, brown the italian sausage while chopping up the potatoes. After the sausage is done browning, add the potatoes and cover; allow to cook for several minutes. Once the potatoes start to tender, add the chicken broth and then cover again and let simmer until the potatoes are fork tender. Next, add the spices and heavy cream and cover again. Once it starts to boil, add shredded kale and let simmer until the kale is softened.
